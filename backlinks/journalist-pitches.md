## Pitch C — Real Estate Investing / Wholesaling Queries

**USE FOR:** Queries about real estate investing, wholesaling, distressed properties, REI trends

---

Hi, I'm Youssef Ahmed, founder of VA Horizon. We work with real estate wholesalers across the U.S. — placing trained VAs to run their cold calling operations and helping them scale from 1-2 deals per month to 4-8+ without the operator being in every conversation.

On [real estate investing / wholesaling]:

**What I see across our client base:**

The operators who scale consistently all have the same structure: high-volume outbound lead generation (cold calling or SMS), a system to follow up with leads over 30-90 days, and a dedicated acquisition manager to handle offer negotiations. Operators who try to do all three themselves hit a ceiling at 1-2 deals per month and stay there.

The math is unforgiving. At 150-200 dials per day (manual, no dialer), a solo operator might generate 3-5 qualified leads per week. At 800-1,000 dials per day with a trained VA and predictive dialer, that same operator is generating 30+ qualified leads per month and can focus exclusively on the high-value decisions (offer strategy, buyer relationships, deal structure).

The wholesale fee data is also often misunderstood: while national averages run $10,000-$20,000 per deal, the variance by market is enormous — $5,000-$15,000 in Detroit and Cleveland vs. $25,000-$75,000 in Miami and LA. Most "how to wholesale" content quotes the national average without that context.
