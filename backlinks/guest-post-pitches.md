# VA Horizon — Guest Post Outreach Templates

Ready-to-send pitch emails for guest posting on high-DR real estate and business publications.

---

## Rules for Sending

- Personalize the [brackets] before every send — editors notice copy-paste pitches immediately
- Attach 2-3 headline options per pitch (editors often prefer choosing their own)
- Include a brief 2-sentence bio that matches the publication's voice
- Do NOT mention other publications you're pitching simultaneously
- Follow up once after 10-14 days if no reply; do not follow up more than twice
