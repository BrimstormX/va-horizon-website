Buyer Context

Why wholesalers look for AK Callers alternatives

They want more than VA placement

AK Callers is evaluated by investors looking for real estate cold calling VA help. That is a valid buying path. The question is how much of the daily management is included after a caller starts: dials, dispositions, call review, lead rules, callback discipline, and CRM updates.

They need a cleaner CRM handoff

A caller can create noise if every conversation lands in a spreadsheet or an unclear pipeline. VA Horizon includes a HighLevel buildout so seller records, tags, stages, tasks, and follow-up rules are ready before the VA starts dialing.

They want real dialer infrastructure

High-volume calling is partly a tools problem. VA Horizon uses Readymode and provides agency access through its own seat scale. That matters for teams that cannot access a 3-5 seat dialer minimum on their own.

They are comparing a shortlist, not a duel

Use this page for a five-option shortlist. Use VA Horizon vs AK Callers for the direct comparison, and use the best real estate cold calling services guide if you are comparing service models.

2

Call Motivated Sellers

Best for investors who want a motivated-seller calling service instead of a VA-centered system.

Call Motivated Sellers is a relevant AK Callers alternative because the buyer intent is similar: more seller conversations. It may be a fit for wholesalers who prefer a service built around motivated-seller outreach and want to compare lead handling directly. Verify current pricing, lead definitions, bilingual options if needed, CRM handoff, call recording access, and who owns follow-up after a seller raises their hand. It is strongest when the service's current workflow matches how your acquisition person already works.

3

REVA Global

Best for teams that want real estate VA support beyond outbound seller calling.

REVA Global is worth comparing when your need is wider than cold calling. A wholesaler may need admin support, lead coordination, CRM cleanup, transaction support, or assistant coverage across multiple parts of the business. That broader scope can be useful. If the only bottleneck is motivated-seller outbound, ask detailed questions about caller sourcing, script experience, daily dial expectations, dialer setup, QA, and replacement. VA Horizon is narrower, which is the advantage when cold calling output is the central issue.

4

MyOutDesk

Best for buyers who want a large dedicated VA company with broad staffing capacity.

MyOutDesk can be a reasonable AK Callers alternative for teams that value a larger staffing brand and dedicated VA placement across multiple roles. It is not limited to wholesaling cold calling, so the buyer should verify exactly what is included for outbound seller campaigns. Ask about real estate cold calling experience, dialer access, CRM implementation, call QA, weekly reporting, and how replacements are handled. Choose it for broad staffing reach. Choose VA Horizon for a more specific cold calling operating system.

5

Freelance callers from Upwork or Fiverr

Best for owners who want to hire directly and already know how to manage cold calling operations.

Freelance marketplaces are the most flexible route, but flexibility adds work. The operator must find candidates, test their calls, provide scripts, purchase or configure the dialer, build the CRM, define dispositions, review recordings, and replace people quickly when performance drops. Freelance can be smart for a founder with time and a proven outbound process. It is risky for a wholesaler who has not yet built the management layer and wants call volume in the next week.
