In Depth

Power Dialer explained

A power dialer calls one record at a time automatically, giving the caller more control than a predictive dialer but less raw volume. In a wholesale operation, the term matters because it connects the seller conversation to a real next step instead of leaving the team with vague notes.

Power dialing can work for warmer follow-up, smaller lists, or campaigns where conversation quality matters more than maximum speed. VA Horizon cares about this because callers, lead managers, and acquisitions teams all need the same language inside the CRM. When the term is tagged correctly, follow-up becomes cleaner, handoffs improve, and the operator can see whether the lead is worth more time.

It is not always the best fit for large cold lists where live connection volume is the main bottleneck.

Example

A lead manager may power dial warm callbacks while a cold caller uses a predictive dialer for fresh absentee owner lists.
