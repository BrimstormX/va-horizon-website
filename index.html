Call samples

Hear how our trained VAs open calls, handle objections, and book appointments for real-estate wholesalers. Little to no accent, clear voice, great results.

Agent Jeffrey Miller — Cold Call (7:09)

Objective: qualify a lead, confirm timeline & motivation, and secure a follow-up appointment for acquisitions. Script: intro → pattern interrupt → qualification → soft close.

Timestamps
  • Tone & pacing: calm pace, confirmation questions, builds context.
  • Objection handling: uses “totally fair” agreement frame, then narrows on condition/timeline instead of price.
  • Compliance: call purpose disclosed; opt-out honored; no promises; DNC respected.
  • Outcome: appointment booked for acquisitions to follow-up, seller understands what he's getting into.

Agent Elijah Cruz — Cold Call (7:27)

Objective: Call homeowner and qualify lead then secure a follow-up appointment for acquisitions. Script: Introduction → Handle Objections → Qualify Lead → Secure Appointment.

Timestamps
  • Objections & Rapport: handling all objections smoothly but respectfully and moving forward with the qualifying questions
  • Framework: focuses on asking price/motivation negotiates and qualifies the lead then asks about his other properties.
  • Outcome: qualified the first lead, got another lead from him, ended call smoothly.

Notes: Both of these agents are Egyptian, we make sure our overseas agents are well trained and have a native-like accent and are able to handle objections smoothly while simultaneously qualifying the lead.
