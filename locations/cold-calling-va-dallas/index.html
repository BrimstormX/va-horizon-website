FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dallas a good market for real estate wholesaling?
Yes. The Dallas-Fort Worth metro is one of the highest-volume wholesale markets in the United States. No state income tax attracts investors, population growth drives demand, and the sheer size of the DFW metro creates thousands of motivated seller opportunities monthly.
What DFW areas produce the most motivated seller leads?
High-yield areas include Mesquite (75149/75150), Garland (75040/75041), Irving (75061/75062), Duncanville (75116), and southern Dallas County. These suburbs have older housing stock, higher absentee owner rates, and active cash buyer demand.
How many dials per day will my Dallas VA make?
Your VA dials on the Readymode predictive dialer hitting 800+ dials per day with 150–200 live connections. In a metro as large as DFW, high dial volume is essential to consistent pipeline.
How fast can I get a VA dialing in Dallas?
VA Horizon onboards in 48–72 hours. After a 15-minute intake call, we configure your CRM, load your lists, set up the dialer, and your VA starts calling within 3 days.
Do I need to bring my own CRM?
No. VA Horizon includes a fully configured HighLevel CRM with a wholesaling pipeline, SMS follow-up sequences, and lead intake automations. Everything is built before your VA dials day one.
What if I'm not happy with my VA?
VA Horizon guarantees 30 qualified leads per month. If the campaign misses that target, we keep dialing at no extra charge or add caller support until the target is reached.
Do you require a long-term contract for Dallas wholesalers?
No. VA Horizon operates month-to-month with a 30-day cancellation notice. No annual commitments or lock-in periods.
