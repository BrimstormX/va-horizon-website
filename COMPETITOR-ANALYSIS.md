## 1. MyOutDesk

**URL:** myoutdesk.com
**Positioning:** "America's #1 Virtual Assistant Service" — general real estate + SMB
**Pricing:** ~$1,750-2,000/month per VA

### SEO Strengths
- Very high domain authority (estimated DR 50+)
- Hundreds of blog posts targeting broad real estate VA queries
- Strong brand recognition; ranks for almost all "real estate VA" head terms
- Detailed service pages with schema
- Active review presence (Google, Clutch, Trustpilot)
- Strong backlink profile from RE industry sites

### SEO Weaknesses
- Broad positioning means weak specificity for wholesaling queries
- Large site = slower page speed
- Content volume means some thin posts in blog
- Not targeting "wholesaling VA" or "cold calling VA wholesalers" specifically

### Keyword Gaps VA Horizon Can Win
- "wholesaling VA" — MyOutDesk targets broad real estate, not specifically wholesalers
- "cold calling VA for wholesalers" — not a primary keyword for them
- "HighLevel CRM for wholesalers" — not in their content
- Any query with "real estate wholesaling" + VA — underserved by MyOutDesk

### Takeaway
Competing head-to-head on "real estate VA" is a multi-year DA battle. Win by owning the **wholesaling** modifier across all service queries first.

## Positioning Recommendation

**VA Horizon's Ownable Position:**
> "The only VA agency built for real estate wholesalers — combining trained accent-neutral cold callers, high-volume SMS blast campaigns, and a HighLevel-powered CRM into one deployable system."

This position is **not claimed by any competitor.** It layers:
1. Niche specificity (wholesalers only — not broad RE, not general staffing)
2. Human + SMS outreach bundle (no competitor combines cold calling VAs with SMS blast)
3. GHL CRM included (no competitor bundles a fully-built CRM with VA services)
4. System/stack (not just a VA, a full outreach + follow-up + pipeline layer)

**Why SMS over AI agent as lead differentiator:**
- SMS blast is a proven, high-ROI channel wholesalers already understand and want
- It directly addresses a concrete pain point (follow-up volume and speed)
- Easier to demonstrate ROI vs. AI agent which requires more buyer education
- No Accent Callers and AK Callers have zero SMS offering — immediate differentiation

This should be the core message across homepage, meta descriptions, OG tags, and all long-form content.
