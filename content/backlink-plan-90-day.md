# VA Horizon 90-Day White-Hat Backlink Execution Plan

**Goal:** Earn 35 to 50 new referring domains in 90 days by promoting VA Horizon's calculators, guides, case studies, founder expertise, and brand profiles.

**Risk rule:** White-hat only. No paid followed links, PBNs, automated link packages, mass guest-post farms, or manipulative reciprocal links.

**Budget assumption:** $1,000 to $3,000/month for prospecting tools, light design/content help, expert-source platforms, and properly tagged sponsorships.

---

## Operating Principles

Google's public guidance is the guardrail for this campaign:

- Build links because they help people discover useful pages, not because a placement can be forced.
- Avoid link spam tactics such as buying links that pass ranking credit, excessive exchanges, automated links, low-quality guest posts, and advertorial links without proper tags.
- Any paid or sponsored placement must use `rel="sponsored"` or `rel="nofollow"`.
- Do not request exact-match commercial anchor text. Let publishers link naturally.

Reference docs:

- Google SEO Starter Guide: https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/seo-starter-guide
- Google Search Spam Policies: https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials/spam-policies

Anchor mix:

- 60% branded or naked URL: `VA Horizon`, `vahorizon.site`, `https://www.vahorizon.site/`

- 5% exact-match commercial anchors max

## Weekly Operating Cadence

Every Monday:

- Choose 2 linkable assets to push that week.
- Add 75 new prospects using `content/backlink-prospecting-queries.csv`.
- Score each prospect with the quality checklist below.

Tuesday through Thursday:

- Send 50 personalized emails or DMs.
- Log every touch in `content/backlink-prospect-tracker.csv`.

Friday:

- Send due follow-ups.
- Check for live links.
- Update link status, anchor text, and link attribute.
- Review ratios against the target backlink mix.

Follow-up timing:

- Follow-up 1: 4 days after first touch.
- Follow-up 2: 9 days after first touch.
- Stop after 3 total touches.

## Quality Scorecard

Only pitch prospects that pass at least 4 of 6 checks:

| Check | Pass Criteria |
|---|---|
| Relevance | Audience includes real estate investors, wholesalers, sales teams, VA buyers, or SMB operators |
| Editorial standards | Site publishes real articles/resources, not thin guest-post inventory |
| Real audience | Has signs of traffic, community, newsletter, podcast, or social activity |
| Link context | VA Horizon asset would genuinely improve the page |
| Link policy | No required paid followed link or mandatory exchange |
| Brand safety | No gambling, adult, payday loan, scraped, AI-spam, or spun-content footprint |

Reject immediately:

- "Write for us" sites with dozens of unrelated topics
- Sites selling DA/DR packages
- Sites requiring exact-match anchors
- Sites demanding a followed paid link
- Directories with no editorial review or real users

Track weekly:

- New referring domains

- Followed/nofollow/sponsored attribute
- Link type
- Referral sessions
- Assisted conversions

90-day success criteria:

- 35-50 new referring domains
- At least 20 links to tools/guides
- At least 8 entity/profile citations
- At least 5 expert quote, podcast, or editorial links
- No more than 5% exact-match commercial anchors
- Zero known spam/PBN/paid-followed links
- 20-30% lift in impressions for linked guide/tool pages
