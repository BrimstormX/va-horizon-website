# VA Horizon Backlink Outreach Templates

Use these as starting points. Every pitch must be edited with one specific detail about the recipient's site, article, podcast, community, or audience.

Do not ask for exact-match anchor text. Do not offer money for followed links. If a placement is paid or sponsored, ask for `rel="sponsored"` or `rel="nofollow"`.

## Reply When Someone Offers a Paid Followed Link

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for explaining the option.

VA Horizon does not buy links that pass ranking credit. If this is a sponsored placement, we can only consider it if the link is tagged with `rel="sponsored"` or `rel="nofollow"`.

If that works on your side, send over the sponsorship details and audience stats. If not, no worries.

Best,
Youssef

## Reply When Someone Asks for a Reciprocal Link

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for the note.

We avoid link swaps where the only reason for the link is SEO. If there is a genuinely useful resource from your site that would help our readers, I am happy to review it editorially, but I cannot promise a reciprocal link as a condition of placement.

Best,
Youssef
