# Brand Entity Setup Playbook

**Goal:** Establish VA Horizon as a recognized entity across the third-party platforms that AI search engines and traditional search engines use to verify legitimacy.

**Why this matters:** Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google's AI Overviews use entity graphs to decide who is real and who is filler. A company with profiles on LinkedIn, G2, Capterra, Crunchbase, and Clutch passes the smell test. A company with only its own website does not.

**Total time investment:** 4 to 6 hours active work. Approvals take 1 to 3 weeks per platform.

## Final notes

- Use the same logo and same tagline across all 5 profiles. Consistency = trust.
- Use the same founder name and same headshot. Mismatches trigger spam filters.
- Reply to every review within 48 hours, positive or negative. Public response demonstrates ownership.
- Update profiles quarterly. Stale information signals an inactive business to crawlers.
- Track inbound from each platform with UTM parameters: `?utm_source=g2&utm_medium=referral` etc. In 6 months you will know which platform actually drives leads vs. which one only serves SEO purposes.
