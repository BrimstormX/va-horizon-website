## Required Data Tables

| Table | Required Fields |
|---|---|
| Daily dial volume | VA role, dialer type, dials/day, talk-time range |
| Contact rate | Market type, time window, answer rate, bad-number rate |
| Qualified lead rate | List type, contacts, qualified leads, qualification rate |
| Appointment rate | Qualified leads, appointments booked, appointment rate |
| Follow-up timing | Follow-up touch number, timing, reply rate, appointment contribution |
| Cost comparison | Managed VA, in-house caller, freelancer, loaded monthly cost |

## Methodology Section

Include:

- Date range of calls included
- Number of accounts/operators represented
- Total calls or dial attempts
- Markets included
- Definition of contact
- Definition of qualified lead
- Definition of appointment
- What was excluded from the sample
- Limitations and likely sources of bias

Do not overstate. If data is directional or sampled, say so.

## Recommended Report Structure

1. Quick answer: 5-7 benchmark bullets
2. Methodology
3. Key benchmark table
4. What changes contact rate
5. What changes qualified lead rate
6. Follow-up benchmarks
7. VA vs in-house cost context

9. Download/share/cite section
