What Counts as a Qualified Lead?

  • You provided the full, unedited chat with the prospect.
  • You asked the qualifying questions below and received a Yes to the first one.
  • You sent the website in the chat and the prospect looked at it (they replied “ok/checked/looking now/yes”).
  • Details you shared are accurate (we verify on the call).
  • The prospect is a real estate wholesaler (preferably new/early‑stage).

Exact Qualifying Questions

“would you be open to hiring VAs”
“Here is our website if you'd like to take a look, vahorizon.site”

Use these exact lines in your outreach. A “Yes” to Q1 + evidence they looked at the site makes the lead eligible for qualification (subject to verification and call).
