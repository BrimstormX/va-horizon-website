Lead Qualification

Qualified Lead Criteria

What Counts as a Qualified Lead?

  • You provided the full, unedited chat with the prospect.
  • You asked the qualifying questions below and received a Yes to the first one.
  • You sent the website in the chat and the prospect looked at it (they replied "ok/checked/looking now/yes").
  • Details you shared are accurate (we verify on the call).
  • The prospect is a real estate wholesaler (preferably new/early-stage).

Exact Qualifying Questions

"would you be open to hiring VAs"
"Here is our website if you'd like to take a look, vahorizon.site"

Use these exact lines in your outreach. A "Yes" to Q1 plus evidence they looked at the site makes the lead eligible for qualification (subject to verification and call).

Integrity: If information you provided is found inaccurate during our call, the lead is disqualified and $5 is deducted from your next lead payment. Any attempt to cheat results in deductions then termination.

Submission Process

How to Submit a Lead

  1. Send both qualifying lines (Q1 + Q2) and get a Yes on Q1.
  2. Ensure the prospect looked at the website (shown in chat).
  3. Share the full unedited chat (screenshots or export).
  4. Include prospect details: name, phone/email, market (if known).
  5. Provide the Calendly booking or the proposed time.
