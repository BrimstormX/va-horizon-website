## Business Overview

**Who:** VA Horizon places trained, accent-neutral virtual assistants with U.S. real estate wholesalers.

**What:** Cold calling VAs, high-volume SMS blast campaigns, HighLevel-powered CRM (built and managed for clients), list pulling, skip tracing, and buyer disposition VAs.

**Differentiators:**
- Built by wholesalers, for wholesalers
- 48-hour deployment, 5-day replacement guarantee
- Accent-neutral callers with niche-specific training (real estate scripts, PropStream, Batch, Pipedrive)
- High-volume SMS blast for follow-up and re-engagement — included with service
- HighLevel CRM fully built and managed for every client

**Target Customer:** U.S.-based real estate wholesalers doing or wanting to scale to 2–10+ deals/month who lack bandwidth for lead generation.

**Sales Model:** Inbound + referral. Pricing starts at ~$1,000/month per VA.
