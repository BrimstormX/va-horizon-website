Our Cold Calling Workflow

Every VA Horizon cold caller follows the same proven process — built specifically for real estate wholesaling, not adapted from a generic sales playbook.

1

List Prep & Skip Tracing

Your VA pulls targeted lists from PropStream or BatchLeads, runs skip tracing to find accurate phone numbers, and cleans the list before a single dial goes out.

2

Outbound Calling

Accent-neutral VAs work your list using your script (or ours). They qualify sellers on motivation, timeline, and condition — and only pass you warm, ready conversations.

3

CRM Entry & Tagging

Every call is logged in HighLevel with disposition tags, seller notes, and next-action tasks — so nothing falls through the cracks between your VA and your acquisitions team.

4

SMS Follow-Up Blast

Leads that didn't answer go into an A2P-compliant SMS follow-up sequence. Re-engagement rates on cold lists improve dramatically with a consistent text cadence running in the background.

5

Warm Lead Handoff

When a seller is motivated and qualified, your VA books the appointment directly into your calendar or transfers the call live. You only talk to sellers who are ready to have the conversation.

6

Weekly Reporting & QA

Every VA is reviewed weekly: dials, contacts, conversion rate, and call recordings spot-checked by our QA team. You receive a summary report so you always know what's working.
