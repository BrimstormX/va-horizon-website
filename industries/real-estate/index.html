The Process

Our Cold Calling Workflow

Your VA handles the calls. We handle list sourcing, skip tracing, CRM buildout, follow-up sequences, weekly QA, and ongoing performance management. Here is exactly how it works.

01.

List Prep & Skip Tracing

We pull targeted lists from PropStream or BatchLeads, run skip tracing to get accurate phone numbers, and clean the data before your VA dials a single number. Your caller never touches this step.

02.

Outbound Calling

Your Egyptian VA dials 800+ leads per day on Readymode. Fluent English, neutral accent, no detectable foreign origin to U.S. ears. They qualify sellers on motivation, timeline, and property condition using your script or ours.

03.

CRM Entry & Tagging

Every call is logged in HighLevel with disposition tags, seller notes, and next-action tasks — nothing falls through the cracks.

04.

SMS Follow-Up Blast

Leads that didn't answer go into an A2P-compliant SMS sequence. Re-engagement rates improve dramatically with a consistent text cadence.

05.

Warm Lead Handoff

When a seller is motivated and qualified, your VA books directly into your calendar or transfers live. You only talk to ready sellers.

06.

Weekly Reporting & QA

Every VA is reviewed weekly: dials, contacts, conversion rate, and call recordings spot-checked by our QA team. You get the report.
