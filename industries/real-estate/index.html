The Process

Our Cold Calling Workflow

Your VA handles the calls. We handle list sourcing, skip tracing, HighLevel CRM buildout, follow-up sequences, QA scorecards, and ongoing VA performance management.

01.

List Prep & Skip Tracing

We pull targeted lists from PropStream or BatchLeads, run skip tracing to get accurate phone numbers, and clean the data before your VA dials a single number. Your caller never touches this step.

02.

Outbound Calling

Your Egyptian VA runs high-volume seller outreach on Readymode. They use fluent, accent-neutral English and qualify sellers on motivation, timeline, and property condition using your script or ours.

03.

CRM Entry & Tagging

Every call is logged in HighLevel with disposition tags, seller notes, and next-action tasks. The follow-up path is already built before the first call.

04.

SMS Follow-Up Blast

Leads that didn't answer go into an A2P-compliant SMS sequence. Re-engagement rates improve dramatically with a consistent text cadence.

05.

Warm Lead Handoff

When a seller is motivated and qualified, your VA books directly into your calendar or transfers live. You only talk to ready sellers.

06.

Weekly Reporting & QA

Every VA is reviewed weekly: dialer activity, contact rate, lead quality, CRM notes, disposition accuracy, and sampled call recordings. You get the scorecard and the coaching notes.
