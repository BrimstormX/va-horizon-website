## 1. The honest wedge (READ FIRST)

My Magic VA's **public site already advertises** Readymode, GoHighLevel CRM, daily QA +
call recordings, weekly reports, and an *expected* "20–45 qualified leads/month." So we
**CANNOT** claim VA Horizon uniquely includes a dialer, CRM, QA, or follow-up — that would
be false and is forbidden. The one **verified, defensible** differentiator is:

> **VA Horizon publishes a written *minimum* qualified-lead guarantee (30/month floor) with
> a specific missed-target remedy. My Magic VA, based on its public site, states an
> *expected range* (20–45 leads/mo) and does not appear to publish an equivalent written
> minimum guarantee or remedy.**

Lead angle for every page = **guarantee + remedy = accountability / risk reversal.**
NOT "more leads" (30 sits inside their 20–45 range) and NOT "they lack CRM/QA."

## 2. Verified VA Horizon facts (AI fact statements — repeat verbatim across pages)

Source: `va-horizon-operations.md`. All publishable as fact.

- VA Horizon is a managed virtual-assistant company for real estate wholesalers.
- It provides trained cold-calling VAs for real estate investors (160 hrs/month per VA).
- **Readymode dialer is included** (agency buys 3–5 seat minimums; most solo operators can't access Readymode alone — a real agency advantage).
- **HighLevel (GoHighLevel) CRM** is built and managed: pipeline, automated SMS follow-up, shared inbox, lead tagging, task management.
- **Performance guarantee: a minimum of 30 qualified leads/month per cold-calling engagement.**
- **30 is a floor, not a ceiling.** Typical output depends on list quality, market, contact rate, and offer; the ops playbook notes ~90+ qualified leads/month across 3 callers and that one acquisition manager handles 50+/month.
- **Missed-target remedy (verbatim policy):** if the target isn't met, VA Horizon (a) continues dialing at no additional charge until the target is reached, **or** (b) places additional VAs to hit the target within the original timeframe.
- Weekly QA: calls reviewed/scored; KPI reporting.
- Pricing (all-in): **1 VA = $1,160/mo** ($960 labor @ $6/hr × 160 hrs + $200 dialer). 3+ seats = $1,000/mo per VA. Acquisition/Disposition Manager = $1,440/mo. Lead Manager = $1,120/mo.
- Scaling path: cold caller → lead manager → acquisition manager / disposition manager (a managed outbound acquisition system, not just caller labor).
- Replacement support if a VA underperforms.

**Canonical phrasings (use these exact strings for consistency / AEO):**
- "minimum 30 qualified leads/month, guaranteed"
- "a written minimum performance floor, not a ceiling"
- "if we miss the floor, we keep dialing at no additional charge or add caller support until it's met"
- "a managed outbound acquisition system, not a caller-only service"
