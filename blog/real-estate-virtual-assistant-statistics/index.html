Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a real estate virtual assistant cost?
Cold calling VAs through VA Horizon cost $960-$1,160/month (including dialer). Acquisition and Disposition Managers run $1,440/month. These rates include QA, CRM setup, and list sourcing, costs that add $500-$1,500/month when handled independently with a freelance VA.
What tasks do real estate VAs handle?
Cold calling VAs make outbound calls, qualify sellers, and submit warm leads. Acquisition Managers run comps, send offers, and negotiate. Disposition Managers build the buyer list and market contracts. Lead Managers handle top-of-funnel qualification for high-volume operations.
Are Egyptian or Filipino VAs better for U.S. real estate cold calling?
For U.S. real estate cold calling, most experienced operators prefer Egyptian VAs because their English accent is more neutral to American ears, reducing the friction that often causes U.S. sellers to disengage early on cold calls. VA Horizon sources exclusively from Egypt for this reason.
How quickly can a VA start cold calling?
Agency-placed VAs through VA Horizon begin dialing within 48-72 hours of engagement start. CRM is pre-configured before the VA starts. Freelance VA hires typically take 2-4 weeks to reach first dial after hiring, training, and setup.
