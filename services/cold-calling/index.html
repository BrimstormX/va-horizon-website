Frequently Asked Questions

How many dials per day can I expect?

Our callers target 200 to 300 dials per 8-hour shift. The exact number varies by list type (mobile vs. landline), contact rate, and average conversation length. Callers with shorter average talk times hit higher dial counts. We report daily dials as part of your performance summary.

Are your VAs accent-neutral?

Yes. Accent neutrality is one of our primary selection criteria. All VA Horizon callers go through an English fluency and accent assessment before being added to our roster. Callers with heavy accents — regardless of skill — are not deployed for U.S. cold calling roles.

Do I need to provide my own list, or can you help with that?

You provide the list (or we can connect you with PropStream or BatchLeads for list pulling). VA Horizon handles everything from the list import into HighLevel through the calling, documentation, and follow-up sequences.

What if my VA underperforms?

Performance issues are addressed through our weekly QA review before they become chronic problems. If a VA isn't responding to coaching after our standard correction process, we replace them within 5 business days. The replacement goes through the same market briefing and onboarding — you don't start from zero.

Can I start with one caller and add more later?

Yes. Most clients start with one or two callers and scale as their pipeline grows. Adding callers is straightforward — the CRM and QA systems are already set up to support multiple callers.

Do you work with operators outside of real estate wholesaling?

Our core focus is real estate wholesaling. We've built our training, scripts, and systems specifically for this workflow. We occasionally work with adjacent businesses (fix-and-flip operators, land investors), but we don't deploy callers for industries outside real estate.
