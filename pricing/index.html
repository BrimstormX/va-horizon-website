FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Are these prices all-in or are there extra fees?
The monthly price covers the VA's 160 hours, the Readymode dialer seat, HighLevel CRM buildout, scripts, training, and weekly QA. The only standard upfront cost is a one-time setup fee paid with your first month. SMS campaigns bill usage past the included 10,000 segments.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No long-term contracts. Engagements run month to month and you can cancel anytime with 30 days' written notice, per our Refund Policy.
How does the 30 qualified leads per month guarantee work?
If a cold calling engagement misses 30 qualified leads in a month, VA Horizon keeps dialing at no additional charge or adds caller support until the target is reached. A qualified lead means confirmed motivation, property details, and an agreed next step, not just a contact.
When does the $1,000 per VA rate apply?
At 3 or more cold calling seats. Three callers is also the natural scale point: roughly 90+ qualified leads a month, which is enough volume to keep a dedicated acquisition manager busy. See the team scaling guide for the full sequence.
What are the payment terms?
Setup fee plus the first month are paid upfront, then monthly billing. Setup fees are non-refundable once onboarding starts. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.
What happens if my VA underperforms?
VA Horizon replaces underperforming VAs within 5 business days at no additional placement cost. Because the scripts, CRM, and campaign brief are already in place, the replacement is productive quickly. You get a new VA, not a refund negotiation.
