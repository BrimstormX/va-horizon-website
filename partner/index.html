FAQs

What exactly do I earn?

You earn 30% of the client’s first month once they start, plus 10% for the next 3 months on active subscriptions.

How do you know I referred them?

Either they state your name on the booking form or you email us their details after the intro. We also support unique links with UTM codes.

When do I get paid?

Payouts are processed Net‑30 after the client’s invoice is paid for the applicable month.

Any rules on promotion?

Be truthful and comply with local disclosure rules (e.g., note you may receive a commission). No brand‑term ad bidding or misleading claims.

Do you have a minimum payout?

Yes, $100. Balances roll over until the threshold is met.

Is there a contract?

We’ll confirm via email and attach program terms. You can stop anytime; earned and approved commissions will still be paid.
