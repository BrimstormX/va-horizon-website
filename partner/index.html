FAQs

How are payouts handled?

Payouts are processed on a Net-30 basis via PayPal or Payoneer. You receive 30% of the first month and 10% recurring for the following 3 months.

How is tracking managed?

We use unique tracking links, or the client can simply mention your name on the booking form. We also accept direct email introductions to ensure attribution.

Who is a good fit for VA Horizon?

Any wholesaler or real estate investor who is scaling and needs help with lead management, cold calling, or CRM automation. If they have leads but no time, we are the fit.

Is there a minimum payout?

Yes, the minimum payout threshold is $100. Balances under $100 will roll over until the threshold is met.
