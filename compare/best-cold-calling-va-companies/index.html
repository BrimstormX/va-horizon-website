How We Scored Each Option

We evaluated each company across five criteria that matter specifically to wholesaling cold calling operations:

  1. Wholesaling specialization — Do they train specifically for real estate investing and wholesaling cold call scripts?

  3. CRM integration — Do they work in HighLevel, REsimpli, Podio, or your existing system?
  4. Onboarding speed — How fast can a VA be live and dialing?
  5. Replacement policy — What happens if the VA underperforms?

Freelance Platforms (Upwork, Fiverr, OnlineJobs.ph)

Self-managed hire — lowest cost, highest operational burden

$400–$900/mo
Estimate — varies widely by platform

Hiring a cold caller directly from Upwork, Fiverr, or OnlineJobs.ph is the cheapest option on paper. You pay hourly ($3–$8/hr depending on experience) with no setup fee and no monthly markup. The tradeoff is everything else: you handle the job posting, vetting, interviewing, trial calls, onboarding, script training, CRM setup, dialer subscription, daily monitoring, QA, and re-hiring if they quit or underperform. For operators already running a full acquisitions team, direct hire can make sense. For anyone who needs a system in place fast, the management overhead usually far exceeds the dollar savings.

Strengths

  • Lowest direct cost
  • Full control over the hire
  • Works if you already have training systems

Limitations

  • No training — you build it from scratch
  • No replacement guarantee
  • High management time (10–20 hrs/week to do right)
  • No CRM, no dialer — all setup is on you
  • High churn — unvetted freelancers quit without notice
