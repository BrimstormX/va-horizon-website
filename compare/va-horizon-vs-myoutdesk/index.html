VA Horizon vs MyOutDesk: Which is Better for Real Estate Wholesalers?

Both companies place virtual assistants for real estate professionals. But if you're a wholesaler who needs a cold caller on Readymode with 150-200 connections and wholesaling-specific scripts, these are two very different products.

Disclosure: VA Horizon is the author of this page. MyOutDesk data is sourced from their public website (myoutdesk.com) as of March 2026. We've aimed to be accurate and fair - verify directly before purchasing from either company.

Where Each Company Wins

1. Cold Calling Specialization

VA Horizon

Every VA is Egyptian with no-accent fluent English and prior real estate cold calling experience. They're trained on wholesaling scripts: pattern interrupts for distressed sellers, objection handling for "not interested" and "I already have a realtor," and lead qualification. VAs dial on Readymode (predictive) and deliver predictive dialer coverage with 150-200 connections from day one. Their job is to call, qualify, and submit. VA Horizon handles list sourcing, skip tracing, CRM buildout, follow-up sequences, QA scorecards, and performance management.

MyOutDesk

MyOutDesk offers ISA (Inside Sales Agent) VAs trained for real estate prospecting. Their cold calling targets buyer/seller leads for agents. That's a different conversation than wholesaling outreach to distressed homeowners. Their training is broad by design, not deep on any single niche.

Wholesaler verdict: If your VA is dialing motivated seller lists and needs to handle "I don't want to sell" and "what are you offering?", you need wholesaling-specific training. General agent ISA scripts don't cover that objection tree.

2. CRM Setup and Integration

VA Horizon

VA Horizon includes a fully configured HighLevel (GHL) CRM with a wholesaling pipeline, lead intake automations, SMS follow-up sequences, and a performance dashboard. The CRM is ready before your VA starts dialing. You don't need to set up any software or buy a separate CRM subscription. VA Horizon handles the CRM buildout and follow-up sequences so your VA can focus on calling.

MyOutDesk

MyOutDesk VAs can work in whatever CRM you already use (Follow Up Boss, BoomTown, kvCORE, etc.). CRM setup is not included. You bring your existing system and the VA learns it. This works well for agents who already have a CRM workflow in place.

Wholesaler verdict: If you don't already have a HighLevel pipeline configured for wholesaling, buying and setting it up yourself adds cost and delays. VA Horizon's included CRM removes that barrier entirely.

3. Onboarding Speed

VA Horizon

48–72 hours from signup to first dial. VA is pre-trained, CRM is configured, and the dialer is set up before they start. You do a 15-minute intake call, we do the setup, your VA starts calling by day 3.

MyOutDesk

Onboarding typically takes 2–4 weeks, which includes matching you with the right VA, conducting interviews, completing training, and integrating with your systems. This is standard for full-service VA agencies with a large roster.

Wholesaler verdict: A 3-week delay in getting a cold caller live is 3 weeks of missed deals. 48–72 hours matters when you're working seasonal lists or a time-sensitive market.

4. Replacement Guarantee

VA Horizon

If you're not satisfied with your VA within the first 5 business days, VA Horizon replaces them at no additional placement cost. You get a replacement VA - not a refund, not a credit - within the same billing cycle. Read the full refund policy here.

MyOutDesk

MyOutDesk has a replacement policy - verify the current terms and timeline directly on their website before purchasing, as policies may change.

5. Track Record

VA Horizon

Founded in 2024 - newer agency with documented client results including a 12%→28% appointment close rate improvement, 18%→92% inbound answer rate, and $135,000 in recovered dormant pipeline from a CRM rebuild. See all case studies →

MyOutDesk

Founded in 2008, MyOutDesk has placed thousands of VAs across real estate and has a strong reputation in the agent/broker community. Their longevity is a genuine advantage for buyers who value stability and scale.

The Bottom Line

Choose VA Horizon if you are a real estate wholesaler who needs:

  • → A trained cold caller dialing 200+ motivated seller leads per day from day 1
  • → A fully configured HighLevel CRM without extra setup cost or time
  • → Onboarding in 48–72 hours instead of 2–4 weeks
  • → A guaranteed replacement within 5 days if the VA underperforms
  • → No long-term contract locking you in

Choose MyOutDesk if you are:

  • → A real estate agent or broker looking for ISA, admin, or marketing VA support
  • → A buyer who values a company with a 15+ year track record and large roster
  • → Not in the wholesaling niche and don't need wholesaling-specific training
