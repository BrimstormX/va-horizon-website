Evaluation Method

How we evaluated each cold calling service

We scored each option on the parts that decide whether seller conversations turn into useful pipeline. We did not treat every provider as the same category. Some companies sell lead delivery. Some answer inbound calls. Some place VAs. Some run outbound campaigns. A wholesaler comparing them needs to know the operating model first.

Operating model

Does the provider run the full outreach system, place staff, answer inbound calls, or sell lead generation?

Wholesaling fit

Does the service understand motivated sellers, property condition, timeline, lead notes, and acquisition handoff?

Tools and ownership

Does the package include dialer access, CRM configuration, call recording, follow-up, and QA?

Accountability

Is there a concrete lead target, replacement path, or management layer when output drops?

2. Call Motivated Sellers

Best for investors who want cold calling handled as a lead delivery service.

Call Motivated Sellers positions itself around delivering motivated and qualified seller leads through cold calling. That makes it a direct option for investors who want outbound activity handled outside their internal team. It is not framed the same way as a dedicated VA seat inside the client's CRM, so buyers should clarify who owns the list strategy, lead criteria, CRM handoff, call recordings, and follow-up after a seller says they may sell.

This is a fair shortlist option for an investor who wants the sales team focused on closing while another provider handles cold calling. It is less ideal for an operator who wants to build a repeatable internal acquisitions machine around dedicated VAs, dialer data, and HighLevel workflow ownership.

3. Call Porter

Best for inbound lead intake and seller appointment setting.

Call Porter is different from most options on this list because its core public positioning is live call answering and lead intake for real estate investors. That matters. If your marketing already produces inbound calls, missed seller calls are expensive. A trained answering team can screen, qualify, and schedule appointments while your acquisitions team focuses on offers.

Call Porter is not the same buying decision as hiring an outbound cold calling engine. It is strongest when you need calls answered, intake standardized, and appointments booked from existing demand. If you need daily outbound volume against seller lists, compare it carefully against outbound-first providers.

Compare VA Horizon vs Call Porter

4. Televista

Best for shoppers comparing managed real estate outbound vendors.

Televista belongs in this comparison because it is commonly evaluated as a managed real estate cold calling option. For buyers, the important question is not only price. It is what the engagement includes. Ask whether the service supplies a dedicated caller, which dialer is used, how leads are qualified, how call quality is reviewed, how CRM notes are delivered, and what happens if lead output misses target.

A managed vendor can be a good fit when you want someone else to operate outbound. VA Horizon is a better fit when you also want HighLevel built, Readymode access included, and a dedicated role path that can expand into acquisition and disposition support.

5. REVA Global

Best for broader real estate VA support beyond cold calling.

REVA Global is a real estate virtual assistant company with service lines that include lead generation and cold calling packages. It is broader than a pure cold calling service, which can be useful for teams that also want administrative, transaction, marketing, or inside sales assistant support. Their public positioning emphasizes trained real estate VAs and a managed placement process.

For wholesaling cold calling specifically, the decision comes down to specialization and systems. VA Horizon is narrower: Egyptian callers, prior real estate cold calling experience, Readymode, HighLevel, weekly QA, and a clear 30 qualified leads per month guarantee.

Compare VA Horizon vs REVA Global

6. Lead Mining Pros

Best for investors who want lead generation and coaching packaged together.

Lead Mining Pros publicly positions around guaranteed leads, done-for-you services, scripts, and coaching. That makes it a different choice from hiring a managed VA team. It may fit investors who want lead flow and guidance together, especially if they are still building their acquisition process.

The tradeoff is control. If your goal is to build a repeatable internal system with dedicated callers, CRM records, call QA, and a role ladder into acquisition and disposition, VA Horizon's model is more directly built for that operating path.
