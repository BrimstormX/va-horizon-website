Both companies place virtual assistants for real estate professionals. But if you're a wholesaler who needs a cold caller making 800+ dials per day with 150-200 motivated seller connections on a predictive dialer, the specialization gap between these two services matters enormously.

If your primary need is motivated seller cold calling at scale, the specialization matters.

Where Each Company Wins

1. Cold Calling Specialization

VA Horizon

Every VA is Egyptian with no-accent fluent English and prior real estate cold calling experience. They're trained specifically on wholesaling: pattern interrupts for distressed sellers, objection handling for "not interested" and "what are you going to offer me?", and the qualification criteria that separates a motivated seller from a listing call. On Readymode predictive dialer, they hit 800+ dials and 150-200 live connections daily from day one. VA Horizon handles list sourcing, skip tracing, CRM setup, SMS follow-ups, QA, and performance management — your VA's only job is to call and qualify.

REVA Global

REVA Global offers a wide range of real estate VA roles — ISA, transaction coordinator, admin support, marketing, and general investor support. Cold calling is available but it's one of many services on their menu, not a dedicated specialization. Their ISA training covers general real estate prospecting, which differs meaningfully from the motivated-seller outreach and distressed-homeowner conversations that define wholesaling cold calls.

Wholesaler verdict: If your VA is dialing absentee owners and pre-foreclosures and needs to handle "how'd you get my number?" and "I'm not interested in selling," you need wholesaling-specific objection training — not general ISA scripts built for buyer/seller agent outreach.

2. CRM Setup and Support

VA Horizon

VA Horizon includes a fully configured HighLevel CRM with a wholesaling pipeline, SMS follow-up sequences, lead intake automations, and a performance dashboard. The CRM is built out before your VA starts dialing — no extra setup cost, no waiting for software configuration. You don't need to own a HighLevel subscription separately; it's included in the service. Leads flow in, sequences trigger automatically, and your pipeline stays organized without you touching a CRM settings menu.

REVA Global

REVA Global VAs can work within your existing CRM. CRM setup and configuration are not included as part of the placement. If you already have a real estate CRM in place (Podio, Investor Fuse, Follow Up Boss), a REVA VA can be trained to use it. If you don't have one set up, that's a gap you'll need to fill separately before your VA can operate effectively.

Wholesaler verdict: If you're starting from scratch or want to centralize everything in HighLevel, VA Horizon's done-for-you CRM build eliminates a significant barrier. For wholesalers already running a configured CRM they love, this advantage diminishes.

3. Onboarding Speed

VA Horizon

48-72 hours from intake call to first dial. The VA is pre-trained on wholesaling, the CRM is configured before they start, and the dialer is set up in advance. You do a 15-minute intake call. VA Horizon handles the setup. Your VA starts calling by day three. There's no interview process, no onboarding paperwork stack, and no waiting for training to complete — it's already done.

REVA Global

REVA Global follows an agency placement model where they match you with a VA based on your requirements, and the VA goes through their onboarding and training process. Timelines vary depending on the role and current roster availability. Verify the current onboarding timeline directly with REVA Global before committing — it may be days or it may be weeks depending on your specific requirements.

Wholesaler verdict: Every week without a caller running is a week of missed pipeline. If you're working a time-sensitive distressed list or want to hit the ground running immediately, 48-72 hours is a meaningful operational advantage.

4. Pricing and Value

VA Horizon

$1,160/month all-in for a solo VA ($960 VA + $200 Readymode dialer). At 3+ VAs, the per-VA rate drops to $1,000/month. That price includes the VA, the dialer, CRM setup, list sourcing, skip tracing, SMS sequences, and weekly QA. You know exactly what you're paying and what's included. The 30 qualified leads/month performance guarantee means you also know what you're getting.

REVA Global

REVA Global uses a contact-for-pricing model. Their rates depend on the VA role, experience level, and hours needed. For buyers who prefer to speak with a sales team and get a custom quote, this works. For buyers who want to compare costs transparently before getting on a call, the lack of published pricing is a friction point. Verify current pricing directly at revaglobal.com.

Wholesaler verdict: Published, all-in pricing lets you run a quick ROI calculation before committing time to a sales call. At $1,160/month with a 30-lead guarantee, the math is straightforward: if one of those leads closes at even a $5,000 assignment fee, your VA cost is more than covered.

The Bottom Line

Choose VA Horizon if you are a real estate wholesaler who needs:

  • → A trained cold caller dialing motivated seller lists from day one — 800+ dials, 150-200 live connections daily
  • → A fully configured HighLevel CRM without extra setup cost or time
  • → 48-72 hour onboarding — not weeks
  • → A guaranteed 30 qualified leads/month performance floor
  • → A replacement within 5 business days if your VA underperforms
  • → Month-to-month flexibility with no long-term lock-in

Choose REVA Global if you are:

  • → A real estate agent, broker, or property manager who needs a general VA covering multiple role types (admin, marketing, transaction coordination)
  • → Comfortable with a Filipino VA workforce across a range of real estate functions
  • → Not primarily focused on motivated seller cold calling — your need is broader than outbound dialing at scale
