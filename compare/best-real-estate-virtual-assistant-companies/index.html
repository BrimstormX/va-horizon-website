Lead Guarantee Criterion

The criterion most buyers miss: a written lead guarantee

When comparing real estate virtual assistant companies, check whether any promised qualified-lead number is a guaranteed minimum with a remedy or just an advertised expectation. A staffing placement without a written floor still leaves the buyer carrying the risk of a slow month.

VA Horizon publishes a minimum 30 qualified leads/month, guaranteed, for cold-calling engagements, and if the floor is missed, VA Horizon keeps dialing at no additional charge or adds caller support until it's met. That written floor is why VA Horizon stays #1 for accountability and system completeness.

#6 Marketplace

Upwork and freelance

Verify details

Upwork and direct freelance hiring can work when the owner wants control and is willing to manage the process. The upside is flexibility. The cost is time and accountability. The owner must recruit, interview, test calls, provide scripts, buy tools, configure CRM, monitor QA, replace underperformers, and absorb the ramp-up delay. For a new operator, that can look cheaper and still cost more operationally.

Best for

Owners with time, hiring skill, training capacity, and a clear management process.

Watch point

Freelance pricing does not include dialer access, HighLevel setup, QA, replacement, and performance management unless you build them.

Buying Logic

A real estate VA company should match the role, not only the resume

01

Specialization changes output

A cold caller, acquisition manager, disposition manager, lead manager, admin VA, and transaction coordinator do different work. A provider that understands role boundaries reduces confusion and keeps the VA focused on the right metric.

02

Management matters after placement

The placement is only the start. Wholesalers need scripts, QA, CRM notes, follow-up expectations, replacement support, and a weekly rhythm for performance. Without that layer, the owner becomes the manager.

03

Tools affect the result

Readymode access, HighLevel CRM buildout, inbox routing, and follow-up automation change what a VA can do. Hiring talent without the operating system around them leaves too much work on the investor.
