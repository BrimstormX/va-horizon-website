Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a HighLevel account already?

No. If you don't have one, we set it up for you as part of onboarding. If you already have a GHL account, we build inside your existing subaccount.

How long does CRM setup take?

Most clients have a fully operational CRM — pipelines, automations, SMS sequences, and VA access — within 48 to 72 hours of onboarding kickoff.

Is the CRM included with my VA package?

Yes. The CRM buildout and ongoing management is included with VA Horizon service plans. You don't pay extra for the setup or the VA management of it.

Can I use the SMS blast feature for my own list?

Yes. We set up A2P-compliant SMS campaigns inside your GHL account so you can blast motivated seller lists, re-engage cold leads, and run follow-up sequences — all from inside the CRM.

What if I'm already using Podio or Pipedrive?

Our VAs are trained on Podio, Pipedrive, Airtable, and Google Sheets. We can operate within your existing system. However, many clients migrate to HighLevel once they see what the built-in SMS and automation stack can do.

How is this different from just hiring a HighLevel VA on Upwork?

A generic HighLevel VA knows the platform but doesn't know wholesaling. Our VAs are trained specifically for real estate wholesaling workflows — MAO calculations, skip tracing, dispo pipelines, seller scripts — and are supervised with weekly QA. You also get a replacement in 5 days if needed, with zero agency runaround.
